# K-Town Bites

K-Town Bites is a Kuwait-focused restaurant and cafe discovery application featuring a curated collection of dining destinations across the country. Every listed location was personally visited and reviewed to provide authentic recommendations based on real experiences.

## Features

* Extensive database of restaurants and cafes in Kuwait
* Personal ratings and recommendations
* Search and browse by location or category
* Detailed information about each venue
* Community-friendly and easy-to-use interface

## Technologies Used

* Flutter
* Firebase
* Dart
* Google AdMob

## Project Overview

K-Town Bites was created to help people discover great food and coffee spots throughout Kuwait. The platform is built around firsthand experiences, with every featured venue personally visited and evaluated to ensure genuine and reliable recommendations. The goal is to provide users with a trusted source for finding their next dining destination.

## Mission

To create a comprehensive and trustworthy guide to Kuwait's restaurant and cafe scene through authentic reviews and real-world experiences.
